This application shows the domestic exports for Singapore from 1980 to 2014 in Singapore dollars (denomination of million dollars). The data is grouped by 6 areas; America, Asia, Europe, Oceania, Africa and European Union. The tab 'Chart' shows the data in a line graph grouped by the area.

The tab 'Data' shows the data in a table format. Both tabs shows data with the filter. The left panel allows for selection of the year range and selection of the region. Both 'Chart' and 'Data' tabs will immediately update based on what was selected in the left panel.
